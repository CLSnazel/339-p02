Measurements

Please enter each of your measurements in the form below. All measurements should be in cenimeters (cm)

Neck Size:

Measure around your neck.

Chest:

Measure around the widest part of your chest.

Waist:

Measure around the thinnest part of your torso

Hip:

Measure around the widest part of your hip

Arm Size:

Measure around your arm near your shoulder and through the arm pit

Arm Length:

With your hand on your hip, measure the length between the top of the shoulder to your wrist

Wrist Size:

Measure around your wrist

Shoulder Width:

Measure the width of your shoulders from end to end

Torso Length:

Measure the length from the top of the shoulders down to the hips
